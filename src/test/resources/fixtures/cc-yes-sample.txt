                        Important Safety Instructions for your YES BANK Credit Card
Dear Cardmember,

At YES BANK, maintaining confidentiality of your information and securing your card transactions to avoid any possible misuse is a
paramount importance to us. Hence, we monitor transactions regularly and may also call you proactively to verify certain
transactions from time to time and have been regularly incurred by you.

We request you to make a note of our Centralized Credit Card transaction monitoring contact number: 1600318172 , 1600313472
from where you may receive such verification call.

Highlighted below are few Safety Tips for your Credit Card:

•     Never share confidential information such as your Card number/Card Expiry date/CVV number/DOB / OTP/ Card PIN/UPI PIN/
      MPIN / Internet banking password with anyone over call, SMS, Email or link, even if they claim to be from YES BANK.

•     Beware of calls that are made in disguise of reward point offers, gift vouchers, holiday coupons, lotteries, job offers, investment
      schemes, refund, KYC / Aadhaar updation, Cashback offers, Electricity bill payment, Card Activation, Pin Set/Reset, Limit
      Enhancement ads posted on social media etc. to seek your confidential card information, Login password or OTP.

•     Never reply to email or SMS that seek personal information. Do not share sensitive financial information on Social Media.

•     Do not hand over your Credit Card to anyone and ensure that card is used in your presence.

•     Please check for https:// when you access your account details through internet banking.

•     Please pay complete attention to the SMS alerts like transaction alerts, OTP sent by the Bank and read complete content
      (transaction amount, Merchant Name) and in case of any discrepancy, immediately report the same to YES BANK.

•     Memorize your PIN/Passwords and destroy the mailers. Change the at regular intervals.

•     Manage your Credit/Debit Card transactions (Enable/Disable transactions and set per transaction limit) for domestic and
      international usage based on your need. The same can be done through YES ONLINE (NetBanking) or iris by YES BANK
      Mobile App or Service Portal.

•     Enable/Disable International usage based on your need. The same can be done through NetBanking or by calling our
      Customer Care number.

•     Please review your Credit Card and Bank Statement regularly and in case of any discrepancy, immediately, report the same to
      the Bank.

•     Please ensure that your latest contact information is updated with the Bank.

For any clarification, please contact our 24*7 YES TOUCH Customer Care on the number mentioned at the back of your card.

You can also write us at yestouchcc@yes.bank.in. If you would like to view any other details regarding your account, please login to
YES BANK Netbanking service at www.yes.bank.in.

We thank you for your patronage and we assure you of our best services at all times.
